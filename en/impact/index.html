SUSU Impact

Every gathering owes something to the planet, too.

As part of our commitment, every SUSU LATAM gathering offsets the carbon footprint it generates — funded by SUSU LATAM, not by sponsors.

Why

We prefer a small, real commitment.

We don't believe in big, empty promises. SUSU Impact is simple: we measure the footprint of each gathering, offset it, and share exactly how we did it here — with real numbers, not marketing estimates. If there's no published result yet, it's because we'd rather wait until it's properly measured before announcing anything.
